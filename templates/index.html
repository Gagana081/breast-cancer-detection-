About this application:

This application uses a deep learning model trained on the Breast Histopathology Images dataset to detect Invasive Ductal Carcinoma (IDC), which is the most common subtype of breast cancer.

For best results, upload high-quality histopathology images. The model will analyze the image and provide a prediction indicating whether cancer cells are present.
